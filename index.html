AboutSobre Gabriel

Gabriel J. Perez

Voice actor, audiobook narrator, author and producer. Founder of Feral Hog Content, Inc. in Jersey City, NJ. Bilingual native (Spanish and English), with Hispanic and New York accents among others.

Lead narrator on the Kolo Kino documentary channel, where his voice has carried more than 20 million views across the top nine episodes. The channel publishes long-form essays on cinema, directors and movies. He's also voiced over 150 radio stations across the United States, and writes the comedy bits he performs that have aired on hundreds of stations. On Audible he has released ten titles, including the original Annotations series of annotated audiobook editions of public-domain classics and original works.

"We don't outsource voices or editing. Every project goes through the same booth."
